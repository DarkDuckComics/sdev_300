About Dark Duck Comics

Our Story

Dark Duck Comics started as a pre-deployment hobby, I grabbed a few books from a comic book store for if there would be downtime.

We started this business because that few comics to pass the time turned into boxes when I got back home. My reading turned into hunting

Family Involvement

Our family plays a crucial role in the business by attending Comic Cons together, organizing boxes, and selling nationwide.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to enjoy the world of comics and pass down that enjoyment to future generations
